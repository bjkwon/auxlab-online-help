Remainder after division (modulo operation)

arg

type

Description

Unit or Value scale

x

SCAL, VCT or AUDIO

n/a

div

SCAL

n/a

Outputs
  • y is the remainder after division of x by div

Notes

Examples
  • mod(17,5) rarr; 2
  • mod(-1,3) rarr; -1
