: (indexing)

a = x(:)

"Serialize" the matrix x and turn to a vector.

Commutative

N/A

Data Types

x

z

VCT

VCT

Notes
  • If x: is a vector, it won't have any effect.

Examples

AUX> x=(1:6).matrix(2)
ans =
1 2 3
4 5 6
AUX> x(:)
ans = 1 2 3 4 5
